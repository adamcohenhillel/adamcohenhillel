Hey, I'm Adam Cohen Hillel

X, LinkedIn, Github, Substack
In short, I'm a builder, since forever. I built apps and games that were used by millions of people. I founded and helped build a few companies. I built many many many products - from games, to cybersecurity, hardware, and AI. I also like to read and write a lot (see below).

A more detailed story: (skip)

  • Started coding when I was 8, then around 12 I started walking around my home town to try and convince small businesses I should build them websites - but no one took me seriously, so I decided to build my own stuff.
  • This led to me founding one of Israel's first major mobile games companies, Gruchka Games (iOS) - reaching 1.5M downloads and 150k MAU at its peak.
  • I then joined the IDF and served in one of the elite cybersecurity units as a system and security engineer. There - I basically learned to set up entire server rooms from scratch and write proper code (check out a funny story of my early coding days here)
  • After my service, I moved to London, UK, and joined Cado Security as the first founding member. I grew with the company from seed to series B, and built their entire engineering team. The company later got acquired by Darktrace for $100M+.
  • I founded Deepen, an AI companion some time before chatgpt came out. We got around ~20k downloads in ~4 months, but I couldn't scale it further, and had a co-founder breakup. But I learned a ton!
  • Moving to San Francisco, I built a bunch of AI x Hardware products at Founders, Inc. including The Ark and Adeus, the first open source AI Wearable (announcement), which then became Based Hardware Omi / Friend Company, to which I'm an founding member & advisor.
  • Pivoting back to cyber, I built SightBear, an offensive AI Security Engineer that autonomously finds and exploits web3 vulnerabilities - but quickly decided it's not what I want to do with my life.

In between all of the above, I built many other things, some of which are listed below.
